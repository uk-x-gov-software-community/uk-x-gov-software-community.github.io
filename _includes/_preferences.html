Cookies for the Cross government Software Community site.

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

We use cookies to store information about how you use this service, such as the pages you visit.

Cookie settings

We use 2 types of cookie. You can choose which cookies you're happy for us to use.

Cookies that measure website use

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use the Check MOT history. We do this to help make sure the site is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements.

Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the pages you visit on the find a HVT service
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the site
  • what you click on while you're visiting the site

Google does not collect or store your personal information, for example your name or address, so this information can't be used to identify who you are. We don't allow Google to use or share our analytics data.

Strictly necessary cookies

The site uses cookies for remembering your analytics choice.

They always need to be on.
